Off-Black Tattoo je certifikované tetovací studio v srdci Prahy na Krakovské ulici. Naši umělci jsou odborníci s vášní pro precizní práci a individuální přístup ke každému klientovi.

Off-Black Tattoo is a certified tattoo studio in the heart of Prague on Krakovská Street. Our artists are certified professionals with a passion for precise work and an individual approach to every client.

We specialize in Old School, Realism, Mini, Fine Line and many other styles. Every tattoo is a unique piece of art created specifically for you.
